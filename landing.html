What Demetra does

Everything your family needs to stay on top of their health

From tracking daily vitals to spotting patterns over weeks and months, Demetra brings your family's health picture into one clear, private view.

Whole-Family Profiles

Track health data for every member of your household — from kids to grandparents — all in one place, without juggling separate apps.

Vitals Graph

Visualize breathing rate, heart rate, blood oxygen, and weight over time in a single interactive chart. Spot trends and correlations at a glance.

Medication & Routine Tracking

Log medications, supplements, and daily health routines for each family member. Never miss a dose or forget who took what.

Health Alerts

Get notified when a reading falls outside a normal range for that family member. Catch early warning signs before they escalate.

Appointment History

Keep a record of doctor visits, test results, and diagnoses for the whole family. Share a clear health timeline with any provider, instantly.

Private by Design

Your family's health data never leaves your device. No cloud uploads, no accounts, no third-party access — ever.
